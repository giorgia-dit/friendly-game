What is Wonka?

This Christmas, the air is filled with magic, laughter, and… the sweet scent of chocolate! Gather 'round, my friends, for a game of mystery, delight, and a sprinkle of holiday magic.

Each of you will receive a special chocolate bar, lovingly crafted with joy and holiday cheer. But beware: hidden among these delicious treats is a golden ticket! The lucky friend who finds the ticket will win a very special Christmas gift. 🎁
The rest of you? You get to savor the chocolate, share the excitement, and bask in the festive fun.

How to play

  • 📦 You can track your own chocolate delivery by pressing the "My delivery" button in the navbar.
  • 🍫 When your package arrives, open it and check for a golden ticket—if you find one, you won the prize!"
  • 🎅 Want to see who else is part of the fun? Check out the "Who's in?" button on the home page or in the navbar.
  • 🎁 Once all the deliveries are completed, you'll be able to discover the prize and the winner (if it’s not you!) by clicking the "Secret Prize" button in the navbar.

So just wait for your chocolate gift, keep your eyes open for the glittering prize, and let’s make this a Christmas to remember!

Good luck, my golden-ticket seekers. Let the magic begin! 🍫✨
