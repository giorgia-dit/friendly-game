What is Wonka?

This Christmas, the air is filled with magic, laughter, and… the sweet scent of chocolate! Gather 'round, my friends, for a game of mystery, delight, and a sprinkle of holiday magic.

Each of you will receive a special chocolate bar, lovingly crafted with joy and festive cheer. But here's the catch: only one of these chocolate bars contains a golden ticket! The lucky friend who finds the ticket will win a very special Christmas gift. 🎁
The rest of you will still enjoy the sweet consolation of chocolate and the thrill of the hunt! 🔍

How to play

  • 📦 You can track your own chocolate delivery by pressing the "My delivery" button in the navbar.
  • 🍫 When your package arrives, open it and check for a golden ticket—if you find one, you won the prize!
  • 🎅 Want to see who else is part of the game? Check out the "Who's in?" button on the home page or in the navbar.
  • 🎁 Once all the deliveries are completed, you'll be able to discover the prize and the winner (if it’s not you!) by clicking the "Secret prize" button in the navbar.
  • 🎲 Remember: the winner is selected at random, so everyone has an equal chance to find the golden ticket!

So, ready to start? Just wait for your chocolate gift, keep your eyes open for the glittering prize, and let’s make this a Christmas to remember!

Good luck, my golden-ticket seekers. Let the magic begin! 🍫✨
