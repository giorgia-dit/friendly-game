Nutritional information on chocolate bars

Chocolate bars received from you were either dark or milk chocolate.

Below you can find the commercial and nutritional information related to both types.

Dark chocolate characteristics

100g Organic Dark Chocolate Verso Natura Equo Conad

The "Verso Natura Conad" line is a range of products designed for the well-being of both the consumer and the environment. It consists of four sublines: EQUO, BIO, VED, and ECO, each designed for conscious and attentive consumers. The organic dark chocolate, part of the EQUO range from Verso Natura, comes from a Fairtrade-certified fair and solidary market, ensuring more equitable trade conditions and opportunities for producers in developing countries to invest in their businesses.

This chocolate bar is naturally gluten-free.

Store the product in a cool, dry place, away from direct heat sources. This will help prevent any food waste.

Sustainability

We support the future:

  • Internal packaging - ALU 41 - Metal collection
  • External packaging - PAP 22 - Paper collection

Check your local municipality's rules and discover more at www.conad.it

Product Details
  • Denomination: Organic extra dark chocolate, gluten-free
  • Brand: VERSO NATURA CONAD / BIO
  • Storage: Store in a cool, dry place.
  • Country of Origin: Cocoa paste obtained from cocoa beans sourced from the Dominican Republic and Peru.
  • Ingredients: Cocoa paste*, cane sugar*, cocoa butter*, vanilla extract* (product contains 70% cocoa or more) (*from organic farming)
    May contain: milk, hazelnuts, almonds, cashews, and pistachios.
Allergens

May contain: Nuts, Cashews, Milk / Contains: Soy
